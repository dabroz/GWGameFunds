# GameFunds

🇵🇱 [Wersja polska](https://github.com/GameDevGrzesiek/GameFunds/blob/main/ReadmePL.md)

Hi! My name is Grzegorz Wątroba and I've been making games since 2010 ([MobyGames](https://www.mobygames.com/person/919153/grzegorz-watroba/), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/watrobagrzesiek/)).

Finding money for a game is a job of its own. Information about publishers, investors and grants is scattered across spreadsheets, Discord servers and dead links — and half of it is outdated the moment you find it. This repository is my attempt to keep it all in one place: a single, maintained database of who funds games and how to approach them, together with my notes on the funding process itself.

As always — these are my notes, not legal or financial advice. Deals differ wildly, so **always verify terms and get a lawyer before signing anything** — ideally one who specializes in games. In Poland a good starting point is the [Gamedev Lawyers Association (GLA)](https://gla.dev/) and the games-focused law firms around it.
